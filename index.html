Welcome to the Colorful World of Paper.io 2!

Paper.io 2 is a free online multiplayer game where you compete to claim territory by drawing lines on a white playing field. Establish your own colored territory while preventing opponents from capturing yours!

How to Play Paper.io 2

  1. Start by entering your player name
  2. Click "Play" on the opening screen
  3. Conquer the map by drawing lines around white areas to claim them as your territory
  4. Eliminate opponents by slicing through their trails to capture their area
  5. Check the right side of the screen to see player positions and rankings
  6. Climb the leaderboard by claiming the most territory and sending your message to the Paper.io world!

Tips for Paper.io 2

  • Always return to your territory - You must close your loop by returning to your colored area, or you'll lose all progress
  • The more territory you color, the higher your score - but don't leave your tail exposed for too long
  • Watch out for other players! If they touch your trail, they can eliminate you and take your territory
  • Don't crash into yourself - Avoid hitting your own trail
  • Track your progress with the stats in the top-left corner showing territory claimed and kills made
